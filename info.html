About me

STXACY is the digital persona of Vik Rathore, a London-based creative transitioning into UX/UI design, web development, and AI tools. Bringing a sharp eye for systems, people, and performance honed across a decade of high-pressure environments.

From coordinating large-scale live events at Glastonbury to coaching athletes and managing logistics operations across the UK, Vik has spent years problem-solving on the fly and leading teams through complexity. Now channelling that same energy into building digital experiences that are intuitive, purposeful and human-first.
